Hire Sas to Work For Your Startup!!!

Hi, my name is Sas Ponnapalli. I am currently a senior at the University of Illinois and I am hungry to take my talents to the startup world. My path to getting involved in startups is a pretty strange one. I started out as a Bioengineer, looking to change the world through medicine and technology. Having a programming background and being the son of two computer scientists, technology always played a big part in my life.

Why I feel like I would be a great fit for a startup:

  • Versatility: I don't just mean versatility in the fact I majored in a completely different field, but rather the fact that I have experience programming in many different web and mobile languages and frameworks, including Java/Android, Python/Django, Ruby. In addition, I have built a variety of applications, from a cancer-diagnosing tool to a Real-Time music playlist engine. I feel that this type of versatility is essential in a startup.

  • Self-Starter: People always ask me, "You're a bioengineer, why are you so interested in programming?". To this I am the type of person who really isn't satisfied using other people's software to solve my problems. I like to customize everything I own from the stereo and speakers in my car to the hardware on my phone. I have this same mentality when I use software. Using someone else's licensed software seems foreign to me. I truly believe that I can only fully understand a product if I played a big role in its development.

  • Vision: I truly feel that I am a finisher in that I treat the last 5 minutes of a project the same way I treat the first 5. With this mentality, I feel as if I am really ready to do some great things. Time truly is a terrible thing to waste and while I am young and hungry, I want to honestly say that I made a difference in the world.
